Idly
idly's are made of rice or rava. these are considered as healthy food as it not made of any oils and contain rich carbs and proteins.
Semolina Upma
Upma is a popular South Indian breakfast made with cream of wheat or semolina flour (called rava or suji locally), veggies, spices and herbs.
Andhra Style Vada
Vada is prepared using black lentils and deep fried in oil. This soul satisfying crispy vada dipped in piping hot sambhar with coconut chutney is one treat you shouldn't deprive yourself of.
Soft and Puffy Puris
Puris are made of wheat flour and fried in oil. It is famous all over India. It is served with the cooked Potato curry.
Sarva Pindi
For a healthy breakfast/snack option Sarva Pindi is an excellent choice as it is cooked with atleast one component of each stage of the food pyramid.
Aloo Paratha
parathas are kind of stable breakfast in north India specially in Punjabi homes.Aloo paratha is most popular Paratha varieties made in home or served in road side dhabas.
Dal Kachori Recipe
these flaky kachoris are made with spiced moong lentils stuffing. kachoris can be made and stored for a couple of days in the fridge. Kachori goes well with tamarind dates chutney, green chutney or red chili garlic chutney.
Poha
Spice up your mundane morning routine with this quick and easy poha recipe. Cooked with onions and potatoes and flavoured with lemon and curry leaves, it's oh so light and perfect for when you don't feel like cooking.
Chicken Dum Biryani
Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating.It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.
